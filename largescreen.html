About Breland Pest Control

Breland Pest Control is a family-owned and operated exterminating business proudly servicing
the Galveston and Harris County areas with both residential and commercial pest control services.
We are committed to protecting our customers the same as we would protect our family and
their health. Not only are we here to protect our customers, but we are also here to protect
the environment by using products that are safe to the customer’s home, business, and pets.
Our technicians are thoroughly trained, certified, bonded, and insured while offering
services to residential, commercial, new construction, and businesses.
